Biography

I am a PhD scholar at MedImg Lab, Centre for Biomedical Engineering, IIT Delhi, under the supervision of Prof. Anup Singh. My research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence in neuroimaging, specifically for post-operative treatment planning and response assessment in brain tumors.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Medical Imaging – IIT Delhi (2023–Present)
  • M.Tech in Communication & Information Technology – NIT Srinagar (2021–2023)
  • B.Tech in Electronics & Communication – NIT Srinagar (2014–2018)

Experience

  • Research Intern, IIT Delhi – Jan 2024 to Jun 2024
    Worked on analysis of brain tumors using multimodal MRI for predicting the MGMT promoter status.
  • Project Coordinator, Rhic Innovation LLP – Jul 2018 to Mar 2021
    Implemented image-based projects such as obstacle avoidance robot.
  • Research Intern, Robotics Research Centre, IIIT-Hyderabad – Jul 2017 to Sep 2017
    Worked on image-based visual servoing techniques.

Research Interests

AI in Medical Imaging, Brain Tumor Segmentation, Treatment Planning, Post-operative MRI Analysis, Response Assessment.
