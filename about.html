Biography

I am a PhD scholar at MedImg Lab, Centre for Biomedical Engineering, IIT Delhi, under the supervision of Prof. Anup Singh. My research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence in neuroimaging, specifically for post-operative treatment planning and response assessment in brain tumors.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Medical Imaging – IIT Delhi (2023–Present), GPA: 10/10
  • M.Tech in Communication & Information Technology – NIT Srinagar (2021–2023), GPA: 9.3/10
  • B.Tech in Electronics & Communication – NIT Srinagar (2014–2018), GPA: 8/10

Research Interests

AI in Medical Imaging, Brain Tumor Segmentation, Treatment Planning, Post-operative MRI Analysis, Response Assessment.
